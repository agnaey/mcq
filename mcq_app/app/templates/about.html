About us

We are a team of passionate engineering students dedicated to leveraging machine learning to simplify content creation. As part of our innovative journey, we have developed an MCQ Generator that automates the process of generating multiple-choice questions from textual content. Our project utilizes advanced natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning algorithms to ensure accuracy, relevance, and efficiency. By hosting our solution on a server, we provide seamless access to educators, students, and content creators, making learning more interactive and accessible. Driven by curiosity and a commitment to technological advancement, we continue to refine our project to enhance user experience and improve the quality of automated question generation.
